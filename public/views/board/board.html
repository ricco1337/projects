WHY A BELT DRIVE SYSTEM?

Our belt­ drive system magnifies the torque* of the motor by 3x, giving you
stronger brakes, faster acceleration, and steeper hill climbing than
hub motors. Belts are also quiet and more precise, giving you a better
ride with more control. *Fancy physics term for how much force is
needed to cause an object to rotate.
